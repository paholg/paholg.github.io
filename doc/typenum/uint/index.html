Structs

UInt

UInt is defined recursevly, where B is the least significant bit and U is the rest of the number. U can be another UInt or UTerm. In order to keep numbers unique, leading zeros are not allowed, so UInt<UTerm, B0> should never show up anywhere ever.
UTerm

The terminating type for UInt, it always comes after the most significant bit.

Traits

Unsigned

This trait is implemented for the all things that a UInt can take as a parameter, which is just UInt and UTerm (used to terminate the UInt). It should not be implemented for anything outside this crate.
