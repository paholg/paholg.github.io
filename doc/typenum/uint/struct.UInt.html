UInt is defined recursevly, where B is the least significant bit and U is the rest of the number. U can be another UInt or UTerm. In order to keep numbers unique, leading zeros are not allowed: UInt<UInt<UTerm, B0>, B1> is not allowed, and UInt<UTerm, B1> should be used instead to represent the number 1.
